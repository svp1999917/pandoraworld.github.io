PANDORA WORLD!

Pandora World is a themed land located within Disney's Animal Kingdom theme park in Florida. It is based on the fictional world of Pandora from the movie Avatar, and was created in collaboration with the film's director, James Cameron. The land features stunning landscapes, including floating mountains and bioluminescent forests, as well as two major attractions: Avatar Flight of Passage and Na'vi River Journey. Guests can also explore the Valley of Mo'ara, interact with the local wildlife, and enjoy themed dining and shopping experiences. Pandora World is a must-visit for fans of the Avatar movie and anyone looking to immerse themselves in a breathtakingly detailed and imaginative world
